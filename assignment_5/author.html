J. R. R. Tolkien

A Breif Summary of the Work & Personal Life of John Ronald Reuel Tolkien

Early Life

Tolkien was born the 3rd of January 1892 into a wealthy, Roman Catholic family. Not long after Tolkien's birth, his father died of Rheumatic fever. For some reason after his father's death Tolkien was like: "Dude, I wanna go to Switzerland...", and so he did. His journey through Switzerland was pretty cool, Tolkien thought. He unsurprisingly drew a lot of inspiration from his journey as illustrated in his books, most prominently The Hobbit.

All of the important life events in Tolkien's life are listed in chronoligical order below:

  1. He is born!
  2. His daddy dies.
  3. He travels to Switzerland to taste cheese :)
  4. An intellectual alliance of four puples called TCBS is formed which he is a member of.
  5. He starts studying at Oxford Univeristy for The Intellectually Unchallenged.
  6. He enlists in the british army as the badass he is.
  7. He gets married!
  8. He gets Trench Fever, and not the fun kind.
  9. He leaves the army and starts teaching at the Univeristy of Leeds for The Intellectually Challenged.
  10. He wants to challenge his intellect again and teaches at Oxford instead.
  11. He translates Beowulf to English!

After all this Tolkien finally starts producing those juicy books of fantasy so many people adore.
